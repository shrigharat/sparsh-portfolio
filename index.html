About Sparsh

Sparsh Santosh Patil, a 11-year-old rising tennis talent from Panvel taluka within the Raigad district, is making a splash with his impressive skills and energetic playing style. Known for his never-giving-up spirit and love for the game, Sparsh has quickly become a formidable presence on the court. His athleticism and ability to adjust set him apart, letting him shine in different matches and against various opponents. Guided by respected coaches and his parent, Sparsh has perfected his skills, consistently showing amazing technique and smart playing. Sparsh's journey is filled with many tournament wins and awards, proving his potential as a future tennis star. Off the court, sparsh is a cheerful kid has a hunger to learn, experiment and explore. With each match, he keeps pushing his limits, showing the true spirit of a champion and paving the way for a thrilling future in tennis.
